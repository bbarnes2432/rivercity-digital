# River City Digital — Image Prompts

Cinematic editorial photography. Quiet, navy-shadowed, cream-highlighted, occasional teal accent. Golden-hour or window light. Slight 35mm grain. Looks like the inside pages of a regional dispatch newspaper, not a stock photo.

**Reference image** for every prompt: `public/assets/hero-the-lou.webp` — attach in Higgsfield. Set the aspect ratio shown. Save with the exact filename.

**Universal negative prompt** (paste into Higgsfield's negative field every time):
```
low quality, watermark, signature, oversaturated, neon, glassmorphism, plastic,
HDR halo, distorted faces, six fingers, blurry, jpeg artifacts, AI sparkle effects,
generic stock photo, crowded, busy, cluttered, identifiable faces close to camera
```

People rule for this set: no faces close to camera, no posed portraits. If a figure is in the frame, they are distant, out-of-focus, turned away, or implied (an empty chair, a half-finished coffee, a coat over a chair back).
